Scott Smallbeck

Author of THE FEED Trilogy

Scott Smallbeck writes dystopian science fiction that explores the intersection of technology, consciousness, and what it means to be human in an increasingly optimized world.

His debut novel, THE FEED: Glitch Protocol, introduces readers to Maya Chen, a content moderator living in a perfectly curated digital paradise who discovers that the reality she's been sold is built on a foundation of exploitation and control.

When he's not writing, Scott lives in Seattle with his wife and their granddaughter. He's currently working on SOURCE CODE, the second book in THE FEED trilogy.
